``` This is a fully self-contained Snake game. Here's what's included: **Gameplay:** - 400×400 canvas with a 20×20 grid - Snake spawns in the center with length 3, moving right - **Arrow keys** or **WASD** to change direction - Food (red circle) spawns randomly, never overlapping the snake - Eating food grows the snake by 1 segment and adds **+10 to the score** - **Game Over** on wall collision or self-collision **Controls:** - **Arrow keys / WASD** to steer - **Space / Enter** (or any movement key) to start/restart **Visual Design (Dark Theme):** - Deep black background (`#0a0a0a`) with subtle grid lines - Snake head is bright green (`#4ade80`) with small **eyes** that follow the movement direction - Body segments use a **gradient fade** from green to darker teal toward the tail - Food is a **red circle** with a soft glow effect - Clean rounded rectangles with `box-shadow` and glow accents - Overlay screens for start and game-over states with pulsing restart hint
